遊戲目標Objective

本遊戲採用生存淘汰制。管理你的桌位與手牌，完成客人的餐點需求，同時避免累積過多的「老闆怒氣值」。 This is a survival-elimination game. Manage your tables and hand, complete customer orders, and avoid accumulating too much Boss Rage.

當你的怒氣值達到或超過上限 ── 你就被老闆即時開除！最後留在場上的玩家獲勝！ When your Rage reaches or exceeds your limit — you are instantly FIRED by the boss! The last player remaining wins!

若中央牌庫先被抽完，則進入「午市大清算」，仍存活的玩家進行最終結算 ── 怒氣值最低者獲勝；若平手，小費最多者勝出。 If the draw pile runs out first, the Lunch Rush Showdown begins — surviving players do a final reckoning: lowest Rage wins; ties broken by most Tips.

遊戲設置Game Setup

1

挑選角色卡Choose Characters

將 6 張角色卡攤開，玩家依序各自選 1 張放在自己面前（建議以年齡最小者優先選）。未被選的角色卡收回盒中。 Lay out all 6 character cards. Each player in turn picks 1 and places it in front of themselves (youngest chooses first). Unchosen cards go back in the box.

2

洗牌組庫Shuffle the Deck

將 96 張遊戲卡（不含角色卡）洗勻，組成中央牌庫，放在中央板的牌庫區。 Shuffle all 96 game cards (not the character cards) and place them on the draw-pile space of the central board.

3

拿取標記Take Tokens

每位玩家拿取 1 組怒氣值標記，怒氣值從 0 開始，放在個人桌墊的怒氣軌上。 Each player takes a Rage token, starting at 0, placed on the Rage track of their mat.

4

擺設桌位Set Up Tables

依照所選角色卡的 🪑 桌位數值，使用個人桌墊上對應數量的桌位接待客人。 Use the number of tables indicated by your character's 🪑 Tables stat on your player mat.

5

抽取手牌Draw Starting Hand

每位玩家從中央牌庫抽取 5 張手牌（手牌上限依角色 ✋ 手勢 而定）。 Each player draws 5 cards from the draw pile (hand limit per character's ✋ Hand stat).

6

決定起始玩家Pick First Player

隨機決定起始玩家，準備開工！ Randomly pick the starting player — time to get to work!

5

遊戲流程Turn Flow

遊戲以回合制進行，從起始玩家開始，依順時針輪流。每位玩家的回合依序分為以下階段： The game is turn-based, starting with the first player and proceeding clockwise. Each player's turn has these phases in order:

階段一：抽牌Phase 1 — Draw

從中央牌庫抽 2 張牌加入手牌。Draw 2 cards from the draw pile into your hand.

若抽牌後手牌超過上限 6 張，必須立即棄牌至 6 張！ If your hand exceeds the 6-card limit after drawing, discard down to 6 immediately!

階段二：行動（可重複執行）Phase 2 — Action (Repeatable)

你可以自由重複執行以下行動，直到不想再行動或沒有合法行動為止： You may freely repeat the following actions until you choose to stop or have no legal action left:

行動Action 說明Description
A. 帶位入座A. Seat a Customer 將 1 張客人卡打出，放到自己的 1 張空桌上。沒有空桌就不能帶位。 Play 1 customer card to one of your empty tables. You cannot seat if no table is free.
B. 湊套上餐B. Serve a Meal 手中已有某位客人需要的完整食材組合？宣告為該客人上餐！
1. 指定 1 位自己桌上的客人
2. 打出符合需求的食材牌
3. 如有需要，可蓋 1 張牌作為百搭（每次上餐限蓋 1 張）
4. 進入「上餐干擾與踢爆流程」 Have the full ingredient set a customer needs? Declare a serving!
1. Target 1 of your seated customers
2. Play matching ingredient cards
3. If needed, play 1 face-down card as a wild (max 1 per serving)
4. Proceed to the "Interference & Bluff-Catch" sequence
C. 使用功能牌C. Play an Action Card 強行帶位：將客人卡放到對手的空桌上
暗中加料：不能單獨使用，須在其他玩家上餐時打出
老闆唔喺度：本回合查勤不計空桌怒氣
執枱神功：與對手互換桌上 1 位客人
客滿（反應型）：取消一次對你的強行帶位 Forced Seating: place a customer at an opponent's empty table
Secret Ingredient: can only be played during another player's serving
Boss is Away: skip empty-table Rage during this Inspection
Table Swap: exchange 1 seated customer with an opponent
Full House (reaction): cancel one Forced Seating against you

E. 花費小費E. Spend Tips 花費 3 小費減少 1 點老闆怒氣值。每回合最多 1 次。（收銀阿姐 蓮姐被動：2 小費 = 1 怒氣。） Spend 3 Tips to reduce Boss Rage by 1. Max once per turn. (Sister Lin's passive: 2 Tips = −1 Rage.)
C+. 發動主動技能C+. Use Active Skill 每回合行動階段中，可發動自己角色卡上的主動技能 最多 1 次，不消耗行動權。部分技能指定於其他階段發動，以卡面為準。 Use your character's active skill at most once during the Action phase, without consuming an action. Some skills must be used in other phases — follow the card text.

階段三：上餐干擾與踢爆流程Phase 3 — Interference & Bluff-Catch

當任一玩家宣告「上餐」時，立刻進入以下流程：Whenever a player declares a serving, immediately run this sequence:

1

暗中加料Secret Ingredients

由當前回合玩家左手邊開始，依順時針順序，其他玩家可各自選擇是否打出最多 1 張「暗中加料」。所有加料牌必須一併納入本次餐點檢查。 Starting left of the current player and going clockwise, each other player may play up to 1 Secret Ingredient. All added cards must be included when checking the meal.

2

踢爆窗口Call-Out Window

只要本次上餐出現任何蓋牌，且效果尚未正式生效，立即開啟踢爆窗口。由左手邊開始，依順時針順序，每位玩家各有一次機會決定是否喊出： If the serving includes any face-down card whose effect hasn't resolved yet, the call-out window opens. Starting left and going clockwise, each player gets one chance to shout:

「你跣我！」"You tricked me!"

踢爆者必須指定 1 張蓋牌翻開。一旦有人踢爆，立刻翻開被指定的蓋牌並結算。每次上餐最多翻 1 張。 The caller must pick 1 face-down card to reveal. Once a call is made, reveal the named card and resolve. Max 1 reveal per serving.

3

踢爆結算Resolve the Call

抓包成功Bluff Caught

翻開的是垃圾、錯誤食材或無效牌 ── 出牌者 +1 怒氣，該牌無效。 The revealed card is trash, a wrong ingredient, or invalid — the player who placed it takes +1 Rage; the card is void.

抓錯了！Wrong Call!

翻開的牌符合宣稱 ── 踢爆者 +1 怒氣，該牌正常生效。 The revealed card matches the claim — the caller takes +1 Rage; the card resolves normally.

4

完成上餐Finish the Serving

若最終餐點符合客人需求 ── 上餐成功 棄牌、得小費、清空桌位。 If the final meal meets the customer's needs — Success discard cards, gain Tips, clear the table.

若不符合需求 ── 上餐失敗 無效牌棄置，其餘合法食材退回手牌，客人留在桌上。 If not — Failure discard invalid cards, return remaining valid ingredients to hand, customer stays at the table.

階段三：查勤Phase 4 — Inspection

每位玩家的第一個回合跳過此階段！ Skip this phase on each player's first turn!

回合結束時，檢查你面前的 2 張桌位：At the end of your turn, check your tables:

每有 1 張空桌 ➜ 立刻 +1 老闆怒氣值！
2 張空桌 = +2 怒氣！ Each empty table ➜ +1 Boss Rage immediately!
2 empty tables = +2 Rage!

若本回合已使用「老闆唔喺度」，則本次查勤不產生怒氣。 If you played "Boss is Away" this turn, Inspection generates no Rage.

階段四：奧客結算Phase 5 — Karen Resolution

完成查勤後，檢查桌上所有尚未完成的客人。每位具有「回合結束時 +1 怒氣」效果的奧客，若此時仍未完成上餐，便各自結算一次效果。 After Inspection, check unserved customers at your tables. Each Karen with an "end-of-turn +1 Rage" effect triggers once if still unserved.

同時有 2 位催單奧客？那就 +2 怒氣值！ Two rush-order Karens at once? That's +2 Rage!

即時淘汰（貫穿全程）Instant Elimination (Always Active)

無論任何階段，怒氣值達到角色卡 🔥 抗壓 數值（預設 5 點） ➜ 立刻被老闆開除！ In any phase, if Rage reaches your character's 🔥 Stress value (default 5) ➜ you're instantly FIRED!
  • 所有手牌放入棄牌區Discard your entire hand
  • 棄置桌上所有客人與餐點Discard all customers and meal cards at your tables
  • 不再參與後續回合Take no further turns
  • 其桌位不再作為可互動區域Your tables are no longer an interactable area
場上只剩最後 1 名玩家 ➜ 該玩家立刻獲勝！ When only 1 player remains ➜ they immediately WIN!

蓋牌吹牛規則Face-Down & Bluffing Rules

在本遊戲中，玩家可以用蓋牌進行欺騙！ In this game, players can deceive each other using face-down cards!

類型Type 說明Description
百搭蓋牌Wild Face-Down 上餐時缺少某個食材？可以將手牌中任意 1 張牌面朝下打出，宣稱它是你所需的食材。每次上餐最多只能蓋 1 張百搭。 Missing an ingredient while serving? Play any 1 card face down and claim it is that ingredient. Max 1 wild face-down per serving.
暗中加料蓋牌Secret Ingredient Face-Down 其他玩家透過「暗中加料」，將 1 張牌面朝下加入你的餐點，宣稱是某種食材或附加內容。 Another player uses Secret Ingredient to add 1 face-down card to your serving, claiming it's a certain ingredient or effect.
踢爆檢查Call-Out Check 所有蓋牌在正式生效前，都可能被其他玩家質疑。踢爆者須指定 1 張蓋牌翻開，每次上餐最多翻 1 張。 Any face-down card may be challenged before it resolves. The caller must name 1 face-down card to reveal; max 1 reveal per serving.

午市大清算Lunch Rush Showdown

當中央牌庫被抽完的瞬間，代表午市結束，遊戲立刻停止！ The moment the draw pile is empty, lunch is over — the game stops immediately!

1

未完成客人懲罰Unserved Customer Penalty

每有 1 位未完成客人 ➜ +1 怒氣值Each unserved customer ➜ +1 Rage

若該客人是奧客 ➜ +2 怒氣值If it's a Karen ➜ +2 Rage

2

淘汰檢查Elimination Check

若有玩家因此怒氣值達到或超過上限，立刻淘汰。 Any player whose Rage now meets or exceeds their limit is immediately eliminated.

3

比較勝負Determine the Winner

  1. 怒氣值最低者獲勝Lowest Rage wins
  2. 若平手，小費最多者獲勝Tie: most Tips wins
  3. 若仍平手，共享勝利Still tied: shared victory
